## Assessment Questionnaire

1. Is a value network model that considers product provenance necessary?
2. Are the elements that are part of the model - actors, activities, objects (products and provenance) - necessary for decision-making on the company's business?
3. Are the arrangements for provenance information sufficiently organized in the models?
4. Do the models generated represent the company's current network?
5. Is the representation of the decision-making problem - meeting the consumer's need through various paths - clear enough in the models?
6. Would visions of value creation paths, which consider the sharing of provenance information, be necessary for decision-making in your company?
7. Are the models presented a good modeling technique to help with decision-making?
